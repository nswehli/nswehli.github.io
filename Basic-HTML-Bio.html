Biography
Nedal Swehli (Diwan Marketing Research, Founder)
His expertise is in designing and managing data collection projects using both quantitative and qualitative research tools. He has extensive experience in implementing focus group studies and household face-to-face surveys. His capabilities include designing research methodologies, stetting research objectives and research questions, managing and training researchers, and overseeing field data collection and present findings. He has vast experience with managing quantitative data collection for perception surveys and opinion poll research, including full sampling and methodical experience. He is also fluent in Arabic and English and has over 6 years of experience with field implementation of face-to-face survey interviews and managing teams of researchers conducting quantitative data collection, including perception surveys in Libya. In addition to his skills and experience in managing research projects, he also as experience in policy development. After the Libyan revolution in 2011, he worked as the project coordinator in the Temporary Financing Mechanism, which is a financing channel created by the international community to funnel funds to the transitional government. Afterwards, he provided advisory as Advisor to the Minister of Culture and Civil Society, Economic Advisor at the Prime Minister Office, and Advisor to the Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation.
